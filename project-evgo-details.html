The Client

EVgo stands at the forefront of the electric vehicle (EV) revolution as a dynamic and rapidly expanding company dedicated to advancing electric mobility solutions. Specializing in EV charging infrastructure, energy services, and cutting-edge technology, EVgo plays a pivotal role in shaping the future of transportation. Their diverse portfolio encompasses comprehensive EV charging solutions, energy management services, and innovative technologies that cater to the evolving needs of drivers, businesses, and municipalities. As the EV market continues to flourish, EVgo remains committed to delivering accessible and efficient charging options across the nation. With an extensive network of charging stations strategically placed in urban centers, highways, workplaces, and residential areas, EVgo ensures that drivers can seamlessly power up their electric vehicles wherever they go.

The Problem

As part of the United States federal government goal of having 50 percent of all new vehicle sales be electric by 2030, the White House is announcing public and private commitments to support America's historic transition to electric vehicles (EV) under the EV Acceleration Challenge. In the midst of the electrification revolution, EVgo recognizes that scaling up electric vehicle (EV) charging infrastructure is essential for widespread adoption. However, the challenges associated with managing and optimizing a diverse network of charging stations can be intricate and multifaceted. As the number of EVs on the road continues to rise, the demand for efficient and reliable charging experiences has surged. Traditional charging networks often face issues like overloading, downtime, user frustration, and unbalanced energy consumption. There is a very clear lack of real-time information about station availability, charging speed, and estimated charging times. At present EV drivers cannot confidently locate and utilize charging stations without unnecessary wait times. As the electric grid consumption surges with EV demand scaling, the need for optimizing the use of renewable energy sources and grid resources, contributing to a more sustainable energy ecosystem is dire. There is a need to balance energy supply and demand, reducing strain on the grid during peak hours.

The Solution

EVgo partnered with Quadratica to develop Optima which is a solution that directly addresses the complexities of managing and optimizing electric vehicle (EV) charging networks. By harnessing the power of advanced technologies and real-time data analysis, Optima revolutionizes the way EV charging stations are operated, ensuring a seamless, efficient, and user-friendly experience for both EV drivers and charging station operators.

By intelligently managing the flow of energy, Optima maximizes the utilization of clean and renewable energy, thereby reducing the carbon footprint of charging operations. It contributes to grid stability by ensuring balanced energy distribution during peak usage, promoting a more sustainable energy ecosystem.

This future-proof approach allows EVgo to keep pace with the growing demand for EV charging infrastructure.

Operational Efficiency

Optima's automation capabilities extend to various operational aspects.
